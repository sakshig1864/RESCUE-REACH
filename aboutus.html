About Rescue Reach

Rescue Reach is a community-focused emergency assistance platform developed to support individuals living with Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s disease.

Patients affected by these conditions may experience memory loss, confusion, disorientation, or difficulty communicating during emergencies. In such situations, identifying the individual and contacting their caregivers quickly becomes critical.

Our platform provides a secure QR-based identification system. When scanned, it instantly displays essential details such as emergency contacts, medical conditions, and caregiver information — helping responders act without delay.

Our Mission

To ensure that no individual suffering from cognitive or motor disorders is left helpless during emergencies.

Our Vision

To build a safer and more compassionate society where technology bridges the gap between vulnerability and immediate support.
